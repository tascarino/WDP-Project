Platform for users to log DND sessions, including things like the players present 
(tagging other users?), the campaign being played, and/or session notes.
The problem being solved is having coherent notes for each session,
so that, when next session comes around, everyone has a good basis for what
happened last time.The intended user is a DND player or group of players.
Users will be able to specify the DND group(s) they're in. They can also can 
post session details by group, can search for sessions by date, name, etc., 
and comment on a session if, for example, the original poster
forgot a detail.
